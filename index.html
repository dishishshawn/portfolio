About Me

Engineering with technical depth and real-world intent.

I’m Shawn Agarwal, an Electrical Engineering student at the University of Oklahoma with a strong interest in robotics, embedded systems, machine learning, semiconductor devices, and applied physics. I enjoy building projects that connect theory to real hardware, whether that means designing circuits, training intelligent systems, or leading technical teams through the full design process.

My experience spans undergraduate research, machine learning, robotics, and electronics. I’ve worked on thin-film materials and device-related research, AI-based data projects, and student engineering systems that combine hardware, software, and problem-solving.

I’m driven by curiosity, technical depth, and the challenge of turning complex ideas into real, working systems. Long term, I hope to continue into graduate study and contribute to advanced research in electronics, quantum materials, or robotics.
